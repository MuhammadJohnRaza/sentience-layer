## 💡 Tips for Great Demo

- **Energy:** Be enthusiastic but not rushed
- **Clarity:** Explain technical terms briefly
- **Visual:** Keep the app/screen in focus
- **Flow:** Smooth transitions between sections
- **Time:** Practice to stay within 5 minutes
- **Backup:** Have screenshots ready if live demo fails

---

## 🚨 Troubleshooting

**If backend is down:**
- App has offline mode with mock data
- Simulation still works locally

**If app crashes:**
- Have backup video recording
- Show architecture diagrams instead

**If time runs over:**
- Skip detailed reasoning chain
- Focus on simulation results
